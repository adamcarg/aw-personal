Why we're not growing fast enough
Problem 1
There isn't enough value to change behaviour
Bank transfer is familiar, free, and top of mind — not because it's better, but because it's already decided before users reach us. The product doesn't yet cover enough of what a private sale actually involves to feel like the obvious alternative.
Problem 2
The platform is built for leads, not transactions
The moments that matter — inspection, negotiation, handover — happen off-platform after enquiry. Buyers and sellers leave and don't come back. Payments is optional, not the default.
Strategic bets — FY27
💎
Value
Give users a reason to stay (and come back) by making carsales the most valuable way to complete a private vehicle deal. Expand the product beyond payment to cover the full private sale — deposit, checkout, record of sale, registration transfer, handover.
🔵
Default
Evolve the carsales experience to stop users leaving the platform after enquiry. Make transaction the natural next step — not a separate product users have to find. The moment of commitment should happen on carsales, not off it.
⚡
Expansion
Scale checkout across carsales — Instant Offer and dealer — then beyond it, to any private sale (marketplace-agnostic). Rails first, experience later; unlocked by Q2 work that lets other surfaces call our services.
